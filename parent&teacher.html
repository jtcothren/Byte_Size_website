Education systems around the world are increasingly addressing the need to integrate Computer Science into their standard curricula. Yet, there are still challenges for kids who want to learn coding, as computer science has not yet made its way into all schools at the rate it is needed out in the workforce. Byte Size can help your kid with their interest in learning to code, safely and in a fun way!

Kids get rewards for continued learning!


Kids will unlock “new ingredients” the more that they learn to code, which lets them level up as a chef and be able to create more complex dishes. Becoming a 5 start chef creating fantastic dishes is just a few lines of code away!



Safely connect with friends and share creations!


Safety is important to us and we know it’s important for parents. We have a secure system where kids can connect with their friends and everyone who uses our app is verified by a parent or guardian. Kids can compete and share their creations with their friends!



Here's how to play


Your have two sous chefs, or cooking assistants, who will help throughout the game. We will give you the basis of all webpages, which is like having a plate to put your food on - every dish needs a plate! Then, throughout the game we give you recipie ingredients to create a simple and then more complex dishes, by putting them together with code. You can share and compete with your friends and increase your chef rating, and make it on the leaderboard!
