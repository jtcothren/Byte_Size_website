What is Coding?


Coding is how we communicate with computers. Writing code is like giving a computer a set of instructions which tell the computer what actions to take. Additionally, you can also give instructions on what colors to use, where certain things go, and how they behave. You can create some pretty cool stuff by learning to code!




Is it difficult?


Coding can be complex. But it is very similar to learning a new language! The cool thing is that coding is a universal language, and it is widely known that the earlier you can learn a new language, the easier it is to learn! Skills for the Future


Computer and Information Technology jobs are growing rapidly in the US and all over the world! As we move more and more into the digital world, the future of people needing to know and understand skills like coding is only going to increase dramatically, and we are already seeing that increase today!


How will my kid learn to code?


By using Byte Size, we aim to teach kids who are around the ages of 7-12, but anyone is welcome to try out our app for an easy way to learn to code! We use food as a medium for understanding the basics of coding - by having and using certain ingredients, you will create different types of food that are easy to make in real life and will be easy to code. We start out with a PB&J sandwich!



Why should I choose Byte Size: Cooking with Code?


Byte Size will give kids a great understanding of coding, starting from the very beginning and gradually increasing their understaning and difficulty level - all while in a fun and familiar style game that lets them become a 5 star chef, creating fun and delicious dishes! These skills will prove beneficial for them for future career possibilities. And the best part, is that Byte Size is 100% free for everyone!
